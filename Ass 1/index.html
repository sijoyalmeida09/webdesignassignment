About Us



Mr. Swapnil Correia CEO

Mr. Swapnil Correia is the founder and Chief Executive Officer of Canadian Life Consultant (MUMBAI). He has completed his Post Graduation from London College of Management & IT, UK. And completed B.Sc. in Hospitality Management from India. He has 17 years of work experience in Hospitality industry. Also, he has an experience in Visa services, Manpower-supply and Foreign Education. After exploring different opportunities, he came up with an idea to establish his own company. Hence, he found an opportunity and support from his Canadian partner, Mrs. Lola Kushakova, Ottawa, Canada, a member of ICCRC (Immigration Consultant of Canada Regulatory council), with her guidance he established his independent firm, Canadian Life Consultant (CLC) in 2017, in Mumbai, India. Mr. Swapnil is extremely driven towards his mission. He is striving to provide the best service to his potential candidates. He is highly committed to provide services in overseas education, immigration and work permit and will always continue to walk on the path of growth and development of candidate’s future, which will lead to develop his own company.
